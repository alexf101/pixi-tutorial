Welcome to Giraffe!

The Giraffe's will mitosis if they eat three apples

When they mitosis, they disappear and two new giraffe's appear in their place. The new giraffe's have a randomly generated neck length in the vicinity of their parent.

When Giraffe's do not eat for awhile, they turn red. A short while later, they will disappear.

You can adjust the height of each tree by clicking and dragging it by its canopy.

You win if you can coax a giraffe to eat an apple for the Great Tree, the large tree in the middle. You lose if all the giraffe's die.

In this way, we see the processes of evolution - natural selection and random variation - at work.
